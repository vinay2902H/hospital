Common Diseases & Treatments:

Disease Name: Influenza (Flu)
Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, muscle aches, fatigue
Treatment: Rest, fluids, antiviral medications (e.g., Tamiflu)

Disease Name: Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
Symptoms: Headaches, dizziness, blurred vision (often asymptomatic)
Treatment: Lifestyle changes (diet, exercise), antihypertensive medications

Disease Name: Diabetes (Type 2)
Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision
Treatment: Diet management, oral medications (e.g., Metformin), insulin therapy

Disease Name: Pneumonia
Symptoms: Chest pain, cough, fever, shortness of breath
Treatment: Antibiotics (for bacterial pneumonia), rest, oxygen therapy if needed

Disease Name: Asthma
Symptoms: Shortness of breath, wheezing, chest tightness
Treatment: Inhalers (bronchodilators), corticosteroids, avoiding triggers

Disease Name: COVID-19
Symptoms: Fever, cough, shortness of breath, loss of taste/smell
Treatment: Isolation, supportive care, antiviral medications (if severe)
